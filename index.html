Wage Calculator

Wage is a regular payment of money you earn on a weekly or daily basis, made by an employer to an employee.

The number of hours worked is the normal amount of working hours, and overtime hours are extra hours worked beyond regular hours, these hours are usually paid more (1.5 more than regular hours).

Your wage on a daily or weekly basis is the amount of hours you work times the amount you get paid ($/hr).
